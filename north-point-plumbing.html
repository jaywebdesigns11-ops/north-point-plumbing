FAQ

Common questions
answered.

Do you offer free estimates?+
Yes — always. Derrick comes to your home, diagnoses the issue, and gives you a clear, upfront quote before any work begins. No pressure, no hidden fees.
Are you licensed and insured in Oregon?+
Absolutely. North Point Plumbing LLC is fully licensed and insured in the State of Oregon. We carry all required certifications and comprehensive liability insurance to protect your property on every job.
How fast do you respond to emergencies?+
Fast. For burst pipes, flooding, or other urgent issues, we aim to be on-site within 1–2 hours in the Albany area. Call (541) 974-7935 any time — day or night, 7 days a week.
Do you work with contractors on new builds?+
Yes. Derrick has extensive new construction experience and coordinates with GCs throughout every phase — rough-in to final trim. All work is inspection-ready.
What brands and materials do you work with?+
We work with all major brands and materials — PEX, copper, CPVC, PVC, and more. We can source virtually any fixture brand and are happy to install customer-supplied fixtures.
Do you handle permits and inspections?+
Yes. We handle the permit process and ensure all work meets Albany and Linn County code. Our work is designed to pass on the first visit — and it consistently does.
